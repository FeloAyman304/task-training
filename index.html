Welcome to The 7th Kuwait Neurology Conference 2024

It gives me an immense pleasure to invite you all to our 7th KNC Conference to be held on December 2024. This is a physical Conference organized after the Covid pandemic and we are coming back after a long period of time. This is one of the most important Conference of Neurology in the Middle East. The Conference is consisting more than 500 participants. We are thankful to The American Academy of Neurology and the speakers for being our inspiration in scheduling this conference. We request all of you to participate in this conference and make it a great success We look forward to welcoming you all again to the KNC Conference. Please mark your calendar for above dates and I wish you all a very successful Conference. Yours Sincerely,
